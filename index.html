Education level & Income & Latte Line

Education level & Income

From the analysis that I did previously, it clearly shows the relationship between education level with income. Where people have a higher level of education, people also make more money. I got this data from the G46 and G17 2016 census results.

Latte Line

New South Wales is largely divided by the latte line which affects many issues. In the southwestern part of New South Wales, there are less people with all levels of education and therefore they make less money. On the other hand the people living in the north-eastern part of New South Wales more people graduated and they usually make more money.

Solution

Therefore, my solution is to establish schools in the areas of New South Wales which have the lowest education. By doing this it will provide similar education for all residents. This should help to reduce income inequality.

One site

To achieve this, I plan to combine all the different levels of education together into one site. This will reduce travel time for people studying. The community of this building will encourage students to study all levels of education because they grow up in the one school.
